Toss It Your Way!

Who
are we?

Tossed Salad Company is a relatively new concept for a fast casual restaurant. Tossed will operate on a very similar basis to other successful fast casual restaurants, such as Moe’s Southwest Grill, Chipotle, Panera Bread, and Chopped.

Why
Tossed?

Not only do we provide a healthy and affordable meal, but we provide it using locally sourced ingredients. So not only does eating a meal at Tossed support your health and lifestyle, but it supports your local community as well! By eating at Tossed, you are doing good things for yourself and your community!

Our
Vision

Our long-term vision is no different from our mission as a whole. We aim to provide the freshest, convenient, most affordable locally sourced meal that one can find in their community. We want you to come eat a meal with us. Come and experience a meal you will never forget and join the Tossed family!
