About Tossed Salad Co.

Our Mission

Here at Tossed, we feel that in an increasingly fast paced and hectic lifestyle that many individuals are finding themselves a part of lately, it is absolutely essential to be able to eat a healthy meal on the go, at an affordable price. With the convenience of so many affordable yet unhealthy dining choices, Tossed aims to fix this by offering a healthy alternative.

With the lifestyles that many individuals are living lately, it is extremely difficult to find healthy eating choices, which are also convenient. This is where Tossed fills the gap.

As college students and members of large families ourselves, we always find ourselves asking “What’s next?”. We typically find ourselves so busy that when it comes down to eating a meal, it comes down to the wire. We all also enjoy eating healthy, but often find that when attempting this, it is always too time consuming to prepare a healthy meal. There are not enough options available offering a healthy and convenient alternative to the convenience of eating unhealthy.

So, we created Tossed as a way for the busy to keep working, and to never stop achieving their dreams!

Why Tossed?

Weekly Specials

Tossed will provide a different specialty salad of the week, every week of the year for a total of 52 specials! With the weekly special, customers will be able to try something new every week of the year and to save money while doing it!

The Machine

Tossed will also provide a specialty juice / shake for every week of the year using their amazing juicer, known to the general public only as “The Machine”. Able to produce up to 45 pieces of fruit a minute, “The Machine” is sure to complement a delicious salad by pairing it with a freshly blended fusion of flavor!

Locally Sourced

Not only do we provide a healthy and affordable meal, but we provide it using locally sourced ingredients. So not only does eating a meal at Tossed support your health and lifestyle, but it supports your local community as well! By eating at Tossed, you are doing good things for yourself and your community!

Home Made

Our homemade salad dressings are unlike any other! While we can’t give you the recipe, even though we would love to, it’s just a secret too great for anyone to bear. Instead, all of our home made salad dressings will be available for you to purchase, so you can bring a little bit of our home into your home!

Tossed Gives Back!

Not only does Tossed use locally sourced ingredients, but it also hosts fundraisers for the community to help support local schools and groups/organizations.
